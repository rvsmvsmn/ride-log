## Major Developments

### 1. Recovery Improved

April often looked like:

> ride hard  
> keep riding hard  
> recover too late

May increasingly looked like:

> load  
> recover  
> rebuild  
> load again

This is arguably the biggest improvement of the month.

---

### 2. Structured Indoor Training Became Established

Indoor riding shifted from:

> recovery spinning

to:

> actual training sessions

Examples:

- Aerobic 12's
- Strength Endurance
- Tempo
- Zone 2

Benefits observed:

- pacing control
- repeatability
- easier progression tracking
- lower emotional variability

---

### 3. Freshness Was Used Intentionally

Late month:

- Form +5
- Form +8
- Form +11

Instead of immediately chasing another giant ride, freshness was used to complete:

- tempo sessions
- strength endurance
- aerobic work

before the May 31 climbing ride.

---

### 4. Better Load Timing

The most important distinction between April and May:

#### April

> fatigue first  
> load second

#### May

> freshness first  
> load second

This resulted in:

- higher quality sessions
- more sustainable fatigue
- better adaptation potential
