## Notes

This was the strongest week since shifting training emphasis toward structured indoor work.

Most of the week was spent accumulating controlled aerobic and muscular endurance volume indoors while preserving freshness for the weekend. The June 7 ride served as a practical test of whether the indoor-focused approach transferred to outdoor climbing performance.

The result was positive. Despite spending significantly less time doing hard outdoor efforts than earlier in the year, outdoor performance remained competitive, resulting in a KOM, several top segment results, and the largest climbing day of the current block.

The week finished deep in fatigue (-29 form), indicating that the next few days should focus on absorbing the workload rather than adding additional high-intensity stress.
