## Reflection

This week functioned almost perfectly as a recovery-to-race transition block.

Monday through Saturday consisted primarily of low-stress commuting and aerobic maintenance work, allowing fatigue to fall while preserving fitness. Form remained positive throughout the week and reached the Fresh Zone before the weekend.

The strategy culminated in a strong performance at the All Roundereur Fondo. The event produced the highest load, highest fitness value, and largest overall training stress recorded so far in 2026.

The primary outcome of the week was not volume accumulation but successful fatigue management leading into a major endurance effort. The resulting fatigue spike (-31 form) indicates the need for recovery during the following week, but the fitness gain validates the approach.
